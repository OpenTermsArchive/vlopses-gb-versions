DPA UK International Transfer Addendum



The Mandatory Clauses of the ICO’s International Data Transfer Addendum (version A1.0, in force as of 21

March 2022) shall apply as published by the ICO at ICO International Data Transfer Addendum, and any later

Transfer Risk Assessment: Supplier shall cooperate reasonably with SHEIN in conducting any Transfer Risk

Assessment required under UK GDPR, and shall provide such technical, organizational, legal or other

information as reasonably requested to assess whether the transfer safeguards will provide protection for

data-subjects’ rights equivalent to those under UK law.
